About Us

Dr. Richard Fernandes, Ph.D, MBA - Chief Executive Officer and Co-Founder

With a first dass honours BSc degree (Sciene), a Ph.D (Biochemistry) and an honours MBA, Luxcel co-fouder and CEO, Dr.Richard S.Fernandes, has spent his entire in the Sciences.

Dr Fernandes has extensive expirience within FDA approved pharmaceutical facillities (Shering Plough, Cork; Leo Laboretories, Dublin, Ireland), in areas such as Analytical Method Development, Validation, Technology Transfer and inmplementing cGMP and cGLP working practices and systems.

Prior to his work in industry, Dr Fernandes held post-doctoral research and lecturing positions in University College Cork. As a post-graduate student in the lab of Prof Tom Cotter, Dr Fernandes speciallized in the field of Apoptosis and published in a number of peer-reciewed journals.
